Welcome!

Buying glasses online is a cinch, thanks to our free Home Try-On program.

01 Browse Our Frames

Pick out five frames from a selection of our favorites. We’ll ship them to you for free.

02 Try Them On

You’ll have 5 days to take ‘em for a spin and ask your friends what they think.

03 Pick the Lucky Winners

Purchase your Home Try-On favorites at warbyparker.com, and we’ll ship them right over.

Just pack up your 5 Home Try-On frames, and ship them back to us. Return shipping is free!
